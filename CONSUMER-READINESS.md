# KiddieSpeech Consumer Readiness

## Short answer

Not yet, but it is moving in the right direction.

## Why not yet

To make a consumer leave Duolingo or any other habit product, KiddieSpeech still needs:
- authenticated parent and child accounts in the running app
- real checkout and subscription activation flows
- actual lesson/session persistence
- stronger daily retention mechanics
- proven speech progress outputs
- trust signals from real usage, not only concept surfaces

## What improved in this pass

- stronger parent dashboard concept
- stronger child session loop concept
- direct comparison surface explaining why families would switch
- feedback-prioritization signals visible in admin
- better product storytelling around speech-specific value

## What must come next

1. real auth and onboarding
2. real checkout buttons into Stripe
3. session persistence and progress history
4. usage-based gating and entitlements in the app
5. a first runnable parent-child flow with Supabase data

## The switch wedge

KiddieSpeech will not win by being “another learning app.”
It can win if it becomes:
- the safest home speech practice app
- the most parent-trusted AI app for kids
- the easiest way to make speech exercises feel fun and repeatable
